## 2. Pricing Tiers & The Complexity Matrix
We scope builds based on "Technical Weight" and "Data Velocity."

| Tier Name | Ideal Profile | Investment |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Operational Audit** | All new partners | Fixed-fee (100% credited to build) |
| **The Foundation** | ~$50k MRR | 3-5 core tools | Starting at **$10,000** |
| **The Growth Engine** | Rapidly Scaling | Multi-region | Starting at **$15,000+** |
| **Enterprise Integration**| 10+ Legacy tools | Custom Middleware | **Custom Quote** |

### Scoping Levers (Complexity Matrix)
The final quote is determined by these four complexity factors:
1. **Node Density**: Standard (3-5 tools) vs. Complex (6+ tools/Legacy NMS).
2. **Data Velocity**: Standard truck rolls vs. High-density fiber/Multi-region deployments.
3. **Legacy Data Debt**: Clean subscriber data vs. "Veteran memory" and siloed spreadsheets.
4. **Team Distribution**: Centralized team vs. Distributed regional managers and partner installers.

## 4. Deep Dive: Target Pain Points & Solutions

### 1. Onboarding & Activation
* **Bottlenecks**: Manual form chasing, preparation gaps for field techs, fragmented data flow.
* **Surgical Solution**: Automated data bridges between Sales (CRM) and Provisioning (NMS) to ensure techs have info *before* the truck rolls.

### 2. Support & Network Ops (NMS)
* **Bottlenecks**: Alerts failing to trigger work orders; unclear ticket ownership; SLA breaches.
* **Surgical Solution**: Logic-based ticket routing that assigns owners based on alert type and tech availability.

### 3. Revenue & Retention
* **Bottlenecks**: High churn due to onboarding friction; siloed data blocking cross-sells.
* **Surgical Solution**: Integrating billing history with support frequency to flag "at-risk" accounts automatically.
